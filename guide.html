Download Guide

Requirements

  • iOS 15 or higher
  • Apple Shortcuts app
  • Minecraft Pocket Edition 1.19+

How to Download

To download Project Lumina, all you have to do is visit the downloads section here. It is recommended to install the latest release. After clicking on your chosen release, scroll down on the release's page to the big white download option. From there, it's pretty clear on what to do. Press the second download option on the shortcut's iCloud page and then get redirected to the Shortcuts app, where you can follow Apple's steps from there.

How to Use

Using Project Lumina is pretty straightforward. To start using the shortcut, simply press the icon in the shortcuts app and choose from the menus. You can access the mod menu, shortcut settings, check for updates, etc. For more help and support on the shortcut, it is recommended to join the discord server or check out the help center!
