FAQ

Q: How do I download Project Lumina?

A: You can download it from the downloads page. If you need help, then refer to our download guide (found in the help center).

Q: What are the requirements for Project Lumina?

A: The requirements is that you need iOS 15 or higher, the Apple Shortcuts app, and Minecraft Pocket Edition 1.19+.
