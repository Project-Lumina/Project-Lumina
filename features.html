Features

Mod Menu

  • ESP - Allows you to see chests and entities through walls.
  • X-Ray - Allows you to see ores through blocks.
  • Invisible Skin - Turns your skin invisible.
  • Armor Tags - Displays what other players are wearing and using.
  • Health Tags - Displays the health of entities.
  • Hitbox Display - Displays the hitboxes of players.
  • Freelook - Lets you view yourself in a 360 degree angle.
  • No Totem - Hides your totem from the screen.
  • Waypoints - Lets you mark anywhere with an armor stand and a locator map.
  • No Pumpkin - Removes the carved pumpkin overlay when on head.
  • Gote Supporter - Adds a clock and compass to the top right of your screen.
  • VSync - Toggles vertical synchronization.
  • Gamma - Changes the in-game brightness (not menus).
  • Username - Changes the non-Xbox username.
  • Smooth Lighting - Toggles smooth lighting. Recommended with X-Ray!

Shortcut Settings

  • Show Notifications - Sends notifications after a successful run.
  • Shortcut Loop - Automatically runs the shortcut again after a successful run.
  • Auto Updates - Automatically checks for updates on shortcut startup.
  • Auto Downloads - Automatically downloads addon packs from the mod menu.
  • Auto Launch - Automatically launches Minecraft after a successful mod menu toggle.
  • Reset - Resets the shortcut back to its original state.

World Editor

  • Icon Changer - Changes world icons.
  • Name Changer - Changes world names.
  • Pack Changer - Changes world names.
  • NBT Editor - Edits NBT data on worlds.
